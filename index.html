Note from the Director

Building the future with Machine Learning and Cryptography

Hackers, renegades, and dreamers welcome. This is a place for the algorithm-obsessed to build the future. If your vision is too big to be told what to do, come here. If you're obsessed with learning algorithms or cryptography, come here. If you want to meet others who are as obsessed as you, come here.

First Pacific AI Labs has three operational capacities: independent algorithm research, startup incubation, and algorithm training. If you are motivated and obsessed, and you want to build the future, we have a program for you. Join us!

San Francisco Incubator

Startup space in Silicon Valley

First Pacific AI Incubator is a 40,000 square-foot office space located in Newark, CA, right across the bay from Facebook and Google. We support startup founders at the pre-seed stage to build their dreams along with 50 of their peers with advanced algorithmic skills in machine learning and cryptography.

Shanghai AI Academy

Training AI professionals with practical skills

First Pacific AI Academy is a 10,000 square-foot dedicated training space in Shanghai China. We teach three levels of 4-week courses. The first level teaches the basics of data science and neural networks including practical use of Tensorflow. The second level teaches autonomous intelligent systems like AlphaGo. The third level is an integrated approach to autonomous distributed systems like self-driving vehicles.
